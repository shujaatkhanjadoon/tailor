## 2. Pain Points (Problem-Agitation-Solution)

### Pain Point 1: Lost Paper Slips

**Agitation:**
- "Kya kabhi aapka paper slip gum hua hai?"
- "Gahak ke order ka record dhundhte dhundhte thak gaye?"
- "Paper slips mein order number, price, delivery date — sab kuch likhna, aur phir dhondhna?"

**Solution:**
- All orders stored digitally forever
- Instant search by name, phone, or order number
- No more lost slips — everything backed up on cloud

### Pain Point 2: Payment Confusion

**Agitation:**
- "Gahak ne kitna diya? Kitna baaki hai?"
- "Pichle mahine ka payment yaad hai?"
- "Aadha diya, aadha baaki — account nahi beth raha?"

**Solution:**
- Automatic advance & balance tracking
- Clear payment history per order & per customer
- One tap to see pending collections

### Pain Point 3: Delivery Date Tracking

**Agitation:**
- "Kab deliver karna tha? Kal ya parson?"
- "Gahak aa gaya lekin kapra tayyar nahi?"
- "Late orders ka pata nahi chalta?"

**Solution:**
- Every order has a clear due date
- Overdue orders highlighted in red
- Auto reminders for upcoming deliveries

### Pain Point 4: Customer Management

**Agitation:**
- "Gahak ka number kahan likha tha?"
- "Usne pichli baar konsa kapra silwaya tha?"
- "Kitne gahak hain? Kaun regular hai?"

**Solution:**
- Complete customer directory with order history
- Search by name or phone instantly
- Track total orders, total spend, last order date

### Pain Point 5: Karigar / Worker Management

**Agitation:**
- "Kis karigar ko kaunsa order diya?"
- "Uske paas kitne order hain? Capacity se zyada to nahi?"
- "Karigar ne order complete kiya ya nahi?"

**Solution:**
- Assign orders to specific karigars
- Track each karigar's workload & capacity
- See order status per karigar in real-time

### Pain Point 6: Manual Reports

**Agitation:**
- "Mahine ka total kitna hua? Aamdani kitni?"
- "Kaunsa kapra zyada silwaya jaata hai?"
- "Business growing hai ya nahi?"

**Solution:**
- Auto-generated daily/monthly revenue reports
- Order trends & popular garments analytics
- Business insights to help you grow

## 9. FAQ Section (SEO Optimized)

### General Questions

**Q1: MeraDarzi kya hai?**
MeraDarzi Pakistan ka tailor management app hai jo tailor shops, boutiques aur darzi ki dukano ko digital banata hai. Orders, gahak, payments aur karigar sab ek app mein manage karein.

**Q2: Kya yeh free hai?**
Haan! Starter plan bilkul free hai aur 30 orders/month tak istemal kar sakte hain. Professional plan Rs 300/month aur Business plan Rs 700/month mein available hai.

**Q3: Kya yeh Urdu mein chal sakta hai?**
Jee haan! MeraDarzi poori tarah English aur Urdu dono mein available hai. Aap apni pasand ki zaban choose kar sakte hain.

**Q4: Kya internet ke bina chalta hai?**
Haan! MeraDarzi offline mode mein bhi kaam karta hai. Internet na ho tab bhi aap orders bana sakte hain, customer add kar sakte hain. Jab internet aaye ga to data automatically sync ho jayega.

**Q5: Kya mobile number ke bina chal sakta hai?**
Nahi, shop registration ke liye Pakistani mobile number zaroori hai. Yeh security aur account recovery ke liye hai.

### Technical Questions

**Q6: Kaise login karein?**
Apna mobile number enter karein, OTP verify karein, aur apna 6-digit PIN set karein. Har baar login PIN se hoga.

**Q7: Kya data safe hai?**
Bilkul safe. Aapka data Supabase cloud server par encrypted store hota hai. Sirf aap apna data dekh sakte hain.

**Q8: Kya mobile app hai?**
MeraDarzi ek Progressive Web App (PWA) hai. Aap browser se directly mobile phone par install kar sakte hain — Play Store ki zaroorat nahi.

**Q9: Kitne devices par chal sakta hai?**
Ek shop account ek hi phone par chalta hai. Agar device change karna hai to naye phone par PIN se login karein, purana auto-logout ho jayega.

**Q10: Kya data export kar sakte hain?**
Haan! Aap apne orders aur customers ka data CSV format mein export kar sakte hain. Orders ke PDF invoices bhi bana sakte hain.

### Billing Questions

**Q11: Payment kaise karein?**
Easypaisa ya JazzCash ke through payment kar sakte hain. Payment screenshot submit karein, admin verify kare ga aur subscription active ho jayegi.

**Q12: Plan upgrade kaise karein?**
Settings > Billing mein ja kar upgrade button se koi bhi plan choose kar sakte hain. Difference amount ka prorata calculate ho ga.

**Q13: Cancel kaise karein?**
Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription se cancel kar sakte hain. Aap current billing period ke end tak services use kar sakte hain.
